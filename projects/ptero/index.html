I designed a typeface inspired by the dichotomy of air travel. It embodies the magic and perceived threat of flying. I created five weights: takeoff, departure, inflight, layover and arrival. These four 24" by 36" poster uses the typeface and tell a story with an emphasis on linguistics. In Greek, ptero means wings, pteromerhano means shake your wings and pteromerhanophobia is the fear of shaking your wings (or flying).
