This book gives visual form to the radio show content of episode No. 339 of NPR's This American Life titled "Break-Up." I infused the book with the emotion of the radio show's audio through the drawings and images. The image sequences of music videos and live performances by popular artists relate to each of the acts, adding another layer of information to the content. Each section is its own signature and they are hand-sewn together.
