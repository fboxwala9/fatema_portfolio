This digital publication gives visual form to the radio show content of episode No. 339 of NPR's This American Life titled "Break-Up." The acts within the episode have sad moments but are humorous and optimistic too. The first act that makes up the bulk of the episode embodies cliches of relationships and break-ups. Romantic vintage comic books similarly portray gender stereotypes in relationships that seem laughable in the present, at least to me. The mood and layout of this publication is derived from the comic book vernacular I discovered in my research, but takes on its own form.
