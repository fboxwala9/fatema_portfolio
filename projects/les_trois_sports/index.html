The triathlon has its beginnings in early 1900s France. Les Trois Sports is the French term for triathlon. I designed a set of three 18" by 24" posters, one for each sport. They are intended to encourage (with humor) my younger brother, Murtuza, who recently ran a marathon to participate in a triathlon. I began by hand sketching, transitioned to designing digitally and then to the letterpress process. On press, I experimented with paint colors and paper colors, shifting patterns to change the composition and layering prints. I especially enjoyed the materiality of letterpress and the uniqueness of each print.
