The Woodward Dream Cruise is a truly Detroit event. It began as a kickback to the nostalgic heydays of the 50s and 60s when cruising along Detroit's iconic Woodward Avenue was a popular past time. I designed a 24" by 36" poster that integrates the event's cruisers and spectators with the welcome traffic and the Michigan U (or Michigan left) traffic symbol. I designed custom type for "Woodward Dream Cruise" and cut out and collaged the cars and lawn chairs together. I drew inspiration from designers Alvin Lustig and Ladislav Sutnar.
