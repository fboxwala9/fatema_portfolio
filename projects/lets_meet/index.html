This project was very cool. "Very" is one of those words that is so commonly used in our language that it feels superfluous. Yet, because of that very reason it begs the question, very what? I designed the four letters by hand, then digitally and finally shaped them with plywood. The four letter words are modular in their construction and decorative. Each letter is approx. 26in X 13in X 2.25in. The process of constructing type out of wood was challenging because of the new tools and techniques that I learned. As a graphic designer, though, I have grown more comfortable with the iterative process towards refinement, in this case a lot of sanding.
