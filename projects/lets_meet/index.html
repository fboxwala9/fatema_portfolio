I was selected to participate in a sponsored project for Compuware along with 6 other designers: Mike Dryden, Randall Bruder, Monica Mitevski, Rachel Peeples, Fatema Boxwala, Jezabel Brasch and Sai Prakash. We were asked to reimagine Compuware's client acquisition process. After weeks of research, we prototyped and designed a presentation tool to link Compuware employees and potential business contacts in order to streamline the communication process and give greater insight into potential services. The application is designed to scan business cards, maintain a comprehensive user database, hold slide libraries that can be edited or updated on the fly per client direction. The application also links Compuware employees to business development leads so that collaboration could take place regardless of the user location.
